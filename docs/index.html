THE STORY

In the mind of every person, there is a corporation, the O-CORP, that watches over the well-being of the subconscious. As a player, you will take on the role of Agent 13, a dream agent who has just started his “dream job." In this job, you will be sent on a multitude of missions deep into the human subconscious in order to eliminate intrusive thoughts, get rid of possible threats, or simply do whatever the corporation asks you to do. As you progress and rise in rank within the organization, you will discover that the dogmas that the O-CORP preaches are not entirely transparent. At the same time, a mysterious entity will appear in your missions and will try to communicate with you to make you see that not everything in O-CORP is what it seems to be.

KEY FEATURES

- Setting: every aspect of our game, from the weapons to the whole scenario, enemies, and characters, is based on the human subconscious of a dreamer.

- Player Movement: in order to create a dynamic, satisfactory, and fast gameplay, the levels will be designed with a multitude of parkour elements, taking advantage of the player's movement skills, to increase adrenaline levels.

- A multitude of weapons with synergies and different qualities: there will be several weapons with very differentiating mechanics that have synergies with other weapons and gameplay features.

- Overflowing Personality: EVERDREAM is characterized by its unique personality, evident in NPCs, weapons, enemies, scenarios, mini-games, and overall aesthetic, providing players with an entertaining and memorable experience.
